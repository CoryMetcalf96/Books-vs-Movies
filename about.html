About this Site:

Welcome to Books vs. Movies - a quick reference comparison between the 'book version' and the 'movie version'. Beyond that, we take a look at full summaries of the books on their own- offering more information than the standard comparison, as well as with the film versions. This was a test at mixing API data within the same program and an effort to troubleshoot between them to increase functionality.
